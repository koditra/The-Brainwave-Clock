# The-Brainwave-Clock
The Brainwave Clock is a smart desktop alarm that jumpstarts your brain in the morning. Choose an educational theme through the companion web server. Solve a chess puzzle, answer a math question, or tackle a science quiz. Wake up your brain every morning.

# Assembly
The first step of assembly is rather easy, we just print out the main fraim and snap the speakers onto place. We then glue on the other components and screw in the screen on the mounts. The cable fits through the slot. The led strip can fit through the other hole.
